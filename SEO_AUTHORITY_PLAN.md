## Step 4) Internal Linking Boost

### Sitewide structure
- Homepage links to all core service pages.
- Each core page links to:
  - 2 sibling service pages
  - 3 relevant blog posts
  - 1 conversion page (pricing/contact/tool)
- Every blog links to:
  - Homepage (branded anchor)
  - 2 core pages (partial-match anchor)
  - 2 related blogs (contextual anchor)

### Anchor strategy (internal)
- 50% descriptive partial-match (`language detection tool for short text`)
- 30% branded (`YourBrand translator`)
- 20% natural generic (`this guide`, `see full tutorial`)

### Link equity flow
- Point strongest backlinks to homepage + 2 cornerstone guides.
- From those pages, funnel links to mid-ranking URLs (positions 10‚Äì20).
- Add ‚ÄúRelated Guides‚Äù blocks on all pages to reduce orphan URLs.

## Step 7) Indexing Boost

### Fast indexing techniques
- Publish updated XML sitemaps for blog, programmatic pages, and core pages.
- Submit changed URLs in Google Search Console immediately after publish.
- Add internal links from already-indexed, high-crawl pages within 24h.
- Request indexing only for pages with substantial unique content.

### URL submission strategy
- Daily: submit 3‚Äì5 highest-priority URLs (new + refreshed).
- Weekly: resubmit pages significantly updated (>20% content refresh).
- Keep canonical tags correct on programmatic pages.

### Ping methods (safe)
- Refresh sitemap `lastmod` accurately.
- Use IndexNow only if supported by your stack/search engines in use.
- Share new URLs on social profiles to trigger crawl discovery.
